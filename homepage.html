Adaptive Hessian-Free Optimisation

This paper reviews optimization challenges in machine learning, with a focus on Machine learning Optimization using the Hessian-Free approach. It explores key optimization techniques, their strengths and limitations within the Hessian-Free framework, and how they apply to various machine learning fields. The paper also addresses open problems and future research opportunities, offering insights to advance both optimization methods and machine learning innovation.

Machine Learning Optimisation, Adaptive Hessian-Free approach
DOI: 10.17148/IJARCCE.2024.13653

Natural Language Processing

This study presents a bibliometric analysis of Natural Language Processing (NLP) research, examining 12,541 publications from the Web of Science database. It identifies key trends, influential journals, articles, authors, institutions, and countries, with the US leading in publications. Major research areas include modeling, neural networks, AI, social media data mining, and learning techniques. The study highlights the growth of NLP research since 1989, with the highest output in 2021 and significant contributions from IEEE Access and Harvard University.

Natural Language processing, Neural Networks
DOI: 10.17148/IJARCCE.2024.13617

As part of the Harmoneynet Talks series, this paper contributes to the broader conversation on blockchain's evolving role and its impact on various industries.

Blockchain architecture, Harmoneynet Talks
